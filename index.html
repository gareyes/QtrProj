Ever since March 2020, the start of the COVID-19 Pandemic, our lives had changed. Many things that were part of our normal day to day routine are now gone, replaced by this "new normal". One of the major things affected was education. FIlipino students are now under distance learning since the current situation does not permit face-to-face classes. This website aims to fill you in on what learning amidst a pandemic is like, how it works, and other related information. Our future is in our children, after all!
